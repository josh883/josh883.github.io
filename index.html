StrategoLight

Welcome to StrategoLight, this is a simple game, but with my own twist based off of the original game. HOW TO PLAY: The rules are simple the game ends when no opposing pieces are left and you can move your pieces any direction one block at a time. Blue will always start first, red second. To move your pieces simply click the desired piece and click the position you want to move to. Please take into account you can only move one piece at a time and can only move one position at a time. After you move it then becomes the opposing players turn. When you finally encounter an opposing players piece then the piece with the highest numerical value will win. Note: no friendly fire allowed and you can only kill the opposing players piece when you move to that pieces position.
